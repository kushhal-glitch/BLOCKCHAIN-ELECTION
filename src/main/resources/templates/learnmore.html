A MESSAGE TO ALL VOTERS FROM THE ELECTION COMMISSION

Standing in the queue and waiting for your turn to cast vote is a part of a history now.

Election Commission of India proposes a new system of voting where everything is transparent and no question of tampering the voting machine as it doesn't exists in first place.

BlockVote is the upcoming way of casting vote where all the votes will be stored in the Blockchain, which makes the system tamper proof and fully transparent.

This is still under various trials! Please expect a few bugs.

HOW TO USE?

1. Make sure that the Ethereum Account privatekey and QR key as been displayed which you've been assigned through GMAIL account.

2. Visit the Cast Vote Page and cast your vote

Regards,

Election Commission of India
